Services and Programs

Dakota Creek Dog Rescue Services has a variety of offerings geared toward the protection and promotion of dogs.
The most notable services and programs are:

Volunteer program

  • Foster care program
  • Community outreach & education
  • Low-cost spay and neuter services through mobile clinic
  • Low-cost pet microchipping

Hours of Operation

  • Operating hours for pet adoptions and reunifications:
  • Monday through Saturday: 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.
  • Sunday: 1 p.m. to 5 p.m.
  • Operating hours for Animal Services clinic:
  • Monday through Friday: 8 a.m. to 6 p.m.
  • Saturday through Sunday: 1 p.m. to 5 p.m.
