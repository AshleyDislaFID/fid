Cant Adopt? Donate!

Dakota Creek Rescue depends on the generosity of our donors and volunteers
Dakota Creek Dog Rescue is a volunteer-powered 501(c)(3) non-profit. We carefully guard every resource we have to maximize the number of animals we can save. Even though our adoption fees do not cover all the costs associated with our rescues, we never want to turn away a dog or cat based on their medical needs, or be forced to end treatment due to cost alone. Instead, we fight tooth and nail to support our animals in every way that we can. We can only do this with your help! Dakota Creek Rescue accepts donations online and by mail to our office at:

Dakota Creek Rescue
2898 Dakota Creek
Yankton, SD 57078

We also accept in kind donations of supplies for our shelter partners. Please contact us to coordinate drop-off or pick-up. If you cannot donate, there are many ways you can help support our mission! Learn more about volunteering your time, or earning money for Lucky Dog by completing online surveys, or even while doing your everyday shopping!
