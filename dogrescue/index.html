Welcome to the Dakota Creek Dog Rescue Website!

Dogs are called "man's best friend" for a reason. They are delightful companions, as well as cherished friends. They have many breeds, are very affectionate, beloved members of the family, and sometimes even protective guardians. Most dogs form tight bonds with their owners, and need to be cared for like the valuable family members they are. Here, we try to give them a nice warm place to call home.
